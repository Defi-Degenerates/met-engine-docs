<Callout> Zero fees on Opening/Closing LP position,swaps(Jupiter takes 0.1% for Ultra swaps) or fees earned by Lping till we are in beta.</Callout>

**1.5% LP Opening Fee** : A flat fee charged when opening an LP position **Manually**. This unlocks access to premium features including **fee claiming**, **wallet tracking**, **position monitoring**, **impermanent loss tracking**, **market cap insights**, and **one-click exits**.

**0% Swap Fees** : All LP transactions use Jupiter Ultra to ensure low slippage and **protection from MEV**.

**0% Fee on Earned Yield** : You keep 100% of the fees generated from your LP positions.

**0% LP Closing Fee** : Close positions, claim rewards, and exit to SOL without any additional charges.
