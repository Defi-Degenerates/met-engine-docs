Met Engine comes with a powerful set of features to make liquidity provisioning easier.

1. <b>Non-Custodial Wallet Generation</b>

   - The bot automatically generates a Solana wallet for you, ensuring you don’t have to share your private key.
   - You can export the private key of the generated wallet and import it into your Solana wallet.

2. <b>GUI based interface to view your position range.</b>
    -  When you use the /position command to check the position, you can see the current range of your position with the binstep.
        ![Image](https://docs.metengine.xyz/range.png)

3. <b>Instant Liquidity Positions</b>

   - Simply enter a CA (Contract Address) and the amount of SOL you want to provide as liquidity.
   - The bot automates swap and creates a position with half amount of SOL and the token swapped.

4. <b>Copy Trading</b>

   - Enter any wallet address to replicate its best-performing positions based on 24-hour yield.
   - Choose amoung one of those positions and copy it instantly with your desired SOL amount.

6. <b>Farm Meteora Points</b>
   - While using the bot and creating positions,you are also farming `$MET points`.
   - Check `MET points` generated by the bot wallet on [Geeklad's website](https://geeklad.github.io/meteora-profit-analysis/).
   - You can export your wallet and claim any airdrops associated with it— your rewards, your control!
