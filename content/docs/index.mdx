### What is MetEngine ?

Met Engine lets you create and manage DLMM (Dynamic Liquidity Market Making) positions on Meteora - instantly, from Telegram, web app, or mobile (coming soon).  You can LP with one tap, mirror top-performing wallets and monitor everything in real time.

### How to Get Started

1. **Get Access**  
   DM [@met_engine](https://x.com/met_engine) on X to request your invite code.

2. **Open the Telegram Bot**  
   Click the “Start” button or use the `/start` command

3. **Top Up Your Wallet**  
   Deposit at least **0.2 SOL** into your Met Engine wallet. This covers Meteora rent (0.05 SOL), swaps, exit fees, and more.

4. **Stay Updated**  
   Follow us across our [socials](https://linktr.ee/metengine) to not miss any updates.
