### What is MetEngine ?

Met Engine lets you create and manage DLMM (Dynamic Liquidity Market Making) positions on Meteora - instantly, from Telegram. You can LP with one tap, mirror top-performing wallets and monitor everything in real time. 
And soon, Met Engine will be **available on web and mobile too**!

### How to Get Started

1. **Get Access**<br/>
DM @met_engine on X to request your invite code.


2. **Open the Telegram Bot**<br/>
Click the “Start” button or use the /start command


3. **Top Up Your Wallet**<br/>
Deposit **at least 0.2 SOL** into your Met Engine wallet. This covers Meteora rent (0.05 SOL), swaps, exit fees, and more.

4. **Stay Updated**<br/>
Follow us across our socials to not miss any updates.
