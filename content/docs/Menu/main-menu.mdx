Currently MetEngine supports following features :
1. **Open Position**: When you want to open a position manually you can use this feature.
    - You need a CA in which you want to open a position.
    - You can open position in any strategy that is supported by Meteora on MetEngine.
2. **Top Pools** : You can discover trending pools from here.
    - We have divided pools into three categories on the basis of the Mcap i.e Degen,Mid-Cap and Blue Chip.
3. **View Positions** : View your current opened positions on the wallet through this. You can close position or claim fees or both through this menu.
4. **Manage Wallet** : All things about your wallet will be here. We also have a custom swapper using which you can swap tokens to sol without leaving the bot.
5. **Honk Index** : Checkout Honk Index and the market conditions using the Honk Index.
6. **Auto Trade** : Everyone's Favourite feature, Auto trade,get started with Auto Copy Lping from here.

<Callout>Main Menu also shows your Bot wallet Address(tap on it to copy) and current Balance for ease of access.</Callout>
